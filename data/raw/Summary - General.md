A Block is a blueprint which guides the preparation, information collection, and production processes, and specifies the information and style requirements for a particular type or pattern of Document. This Block is a “Composition Block”, which will request a combination of answers to questions and requests for sub-Documents (Block Requests) that will serve as the context and components necessary to synthesize a new Document. Provided here is a definition of this Block and the Document it specifies, and step by step instructions to produce the Document. 
The Block “Summary - General” is defined as follows:
A Summary - General Block is used to document and communicate the broader implications and potential impacts of a project or approach. This Block serves as a crucial tool for project leads to articulate their project's significance, approach, and expected outcomes in a clear, concise format. The Block is designed to be straightforward to produce, requiring only the project lead's inherent knowledge and understanding of their own well-conceived project, without the need for additional research or external consultation. While the information gathering process is relatively simple, the challenge lies in effectively synthesizing complex project implications into a single, coherent paragraph. The Block helps ensure that project leads have thoroughly considered their project's main ideas and potential impacts, making it particularly valuable in the early stages of project development. This documentation can then serve as a foundation for various purposes, such as project proposals or team communications.
